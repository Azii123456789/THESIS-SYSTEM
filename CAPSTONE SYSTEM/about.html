About Sangguniang Kabataan

Empowering the Youth, Building the Future.

Our Mission

To provide a platform for the youth to actively participate in governance, nation-building, and community development. We aim to be transparent, accountable, and responsive to the needs of the Katipunan ng Kabataan.

Our Vision

A creative, empowered, and vibrant youth sector that promotes sustainable development and good governance.

Mandate

The Sangguniang Kabataan (SK) is the governing body of the youth assembly (Katipunan ng Kabataan) in every barangay. It creates programs and policies that address the special needs of the youth and ensure their meaningful participation in society.
